Leadership & Involvement

Gonzaga Women in Business (GWIB)

I am a Social Committee Member in the Gonzaga Women in Business (GWIB) club. Through this organization, I attend weekly meetings and events that focus on professional growth, netowrking, and leadership development

GWIB focuses on several key areas that help members develop both personally and professionally:

  • Professional Exposure & Partnerships: GWIB collaborates with well-known companies and hosts guest speakers while highlighting partnerships through social media and campus events.
  • Community & Empowerment: The organization promotes a supportive environment where women encourage and uplift one another academically, professionally, and personally.
  • Leadership Development: Members develop leadership and mentorship skills by participating in committees, supporting peers, and building strong relationships within the club.
  • Growth Pipeline: Committee roles provide hands-on experience and can lead to future opportunities such as chair positions or executive board roles.
